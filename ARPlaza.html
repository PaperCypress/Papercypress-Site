ARPlaza

Client needed to convert a native AR app to Unity. ARPlaza is a AR exhibition platform that uses Light Generation, a mid-range outdoor positioning solution. The final Unity app features Addressables support for modular exhibition updates and in-app positioning tools.

Role in this project

Full stack development, Analytics, Android & iOS plugins(custom wifi funtionality)

Shark On Air

An AR exhibit located at QingShui Rest Station, Taichung. The exhibit includes 4 sections that spans across the property and a main show in the sky that is visible on the grounds.


The main show is a GPS positioned, realtime animation in AR, three minutes in length. It follows a schedule based on real world time, with visual changes depending on time of day.

Role in this project

programming, in-engine rigging & animation, lighting and visual effects.
