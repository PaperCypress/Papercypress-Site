Heineken 0.0 Racing

VR Racing Game

Project type

Client Work

Date

June 2020

Category

Interactive

Status

Completed

Heineken Racing is a racing game developed for VR and Thrustmaster equipment. It was used at Heineken 0.0's, their alcohol-free product, promotional pop-up booth located in Taipei's Xiangti Avenue Plaza.


The game features steering wheel feedback, local leaderboards, as well as scoring mechanics such as near-miss and high speed bonuses.

Role in this project

programming, lighting and visual effects.
