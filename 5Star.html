5 Star Aquarium is small toy where you can enjoy watching soup ingredients swim like live sealife in a pot, before boiling them and viewing your creation displayed on a plate.

It is a project designed to invoke mixed feelings by blurring the lines between aquarium sealife and soup ingredients.

Gameplay

Choose ingredients and soup stock to add to the pot, decorating and populating it as the player wishes. Pressing the "boil" button will instantly cook the items in the pot and display your soup, rewarding cash to purchase more ingredients.

Role in this project

programming, lighting and visual effects.
