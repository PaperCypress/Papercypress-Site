The Patch is a short puzzle game created for the Great Autumn Game Jam: 2021.

The game can be played here https://papercypress.itch.io/the-patch

Gameplay

遊玩

Attract animals onto the patch by fulfilling conditions for each animal Conditions are calculated at the start of the next cycle, so prepare accordingly Animals and crops turn into each other when  half a day passes Unlimited carrot seeds are in the inventory, which grow into mature carrots in a full day when placed
